    Hello! My name is Ben Moldstad, and I am currently in my second year as a Mathematics Professor at Mount Marty Univesity in Yankton, South Dakota. My PhD thesis was on identifying that a circle action on an object in a stable ∞-category is a degree 1 operator ∂ with an identification of ∂2 with the hopf element. This research as now led into my current work, which involves trying to identify the Morita-type of Reedy categories.
